About us

Garkuwa FM 95.5 Mhz Sokoto is a vibrant radio station that came live on air on February 1, 2017, with a mission to serve as the voice of the people without political affiliations and to excel over other radio stations in Sokoto State by showing high professional standards in broadcasting.

The station operates on 2 x 5kw transmitters on 95.5 Mhz frequency modulation, covering the entire Sokoto State in clear signals. Their programs are targeted to encourage good governance and enhance cohesion among the people.

Audience

Garkuwa FM has a unique blend of programming tailored to the needs of their listeners, which includes 75% Hausa and 25% English, Yoruba, Fulfulde, and Ibo. The station has a large listenership not only in Sokoto State but also in adjacent Kebbi and Zamfara States, as well as throughout Nigeria and the world through Tune-In, Radio Garden, Facebook, and Google Playstore.

Our Difference

The station prides itself on giving quality service and adding value to customers. They have fantastic discount offers and are proud to have maintained a reputation as a dependable partner with several organizations in their Advocacy Programmes in Sokoto State and beyond for several years.
